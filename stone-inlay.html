Stone Inlay

Artistic Stone Inlay Work for Timeless Designs

Stone inlay is an ancient craft that involves embedding colored stones into a base to create intricate patterns, floral motifs, or geometric designs. At Jangid Stone & Interior, we take pride in offering customized stone inlay work, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design sensibilities.

Our Specialization in Stone Inlay Work

  • Floor Inlay Patterns – Elevate the elegance of foyers, lobbies, and halls with bespoke inlaid flooring.
  • Wall Art & Murals – Unique inlaid designs on walls create a luxurious visual impact.
  • Furniture Inlay – Custom inlay work on tables, countertops, and decorative furniture pieces.
  • Religious & Monumental Inlays – Detailed stone inlay work for temples, churches, and heritage sites.
