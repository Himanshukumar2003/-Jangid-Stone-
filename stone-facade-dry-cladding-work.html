Stone Façade Dry Cladding Work

A Durable & Elegant Exterior Solution

Stone façade dry cladding is a modern technique that enhances the aesthetic appeal and durability of buildings. Unlike traditional wet cladding, this method uses mechanical anchors instead of adhesives, ensuring better weather resistance, thermal insulation, and long-term strength.

Why Choose Dry Cladding?

  • Crack-Free & Low Maintenance – No risk of peeling or cracks over time.
  • Weather & Moisture Resistant – Protects against rain, heat, and humidity.
  • Improved Insulation – Reduces heat absorption, keeping interiors cooler.
  • Easy Replacement – Individual stones can be replaced without affecting the whole structure.
